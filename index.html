Emotem, a Twitch Extension

TwitchCon Hackathon 2019

Led a team of four to create Emotem, a Twitch extension that generated a emote totem pole/timeline of streamer highlights at TwitchCon’s 2019 hackathon. Streamer highlights were detected automatically from spikes of activity in chat and popular emotes would appear on screen for viewers to vote on. The most voted emote got added to the top of the totem pole along with information about the highlight. The extension was created with Node.js, Firebase, and JavaScript.
